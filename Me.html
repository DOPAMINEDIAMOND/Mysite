Who I Am

✦More than what
the grades say.

My name is Nasrallah. I study LMD Physics in Oran, Algeria — but that barely describes me. I'm the kind of person who writes poetry at fajr, analyzes chess games for fun, reads manga between revision sessions, and feeds stray cats in dorm hallways.

I grew up in Remchi, Tlemcen, the kind of quiet place that teaches you to find richness inside yourself. I learned early that the most interesting conversations happen at night, and that coffee isn't just a drink — it's a symbol of who I am when I'm most myself.

I've been through real struggle. Academically difficult years, loneliness, self-doubt. But I kept writing. Kept praying. Kept moving. Not because I was fearless — but because stopping felt worse than continuing.

"Life keeps moving and I keep evolving. I only failed academically but that doesn't stop me."

I believe in depth over performance. I'd rather have one real conversation than a hundred shallow ones. I help people not because it looks good, but because something in me genuinely needs to.

Spirituality × Internal Motivation × Existence
= The Real Nasrallah  ·  My golden formula

Luna came back
+

"If she found her way to me today, she can find her way back again."
